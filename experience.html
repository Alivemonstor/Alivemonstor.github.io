My Experience

I’m currently in my final year of college, my course being games development and coding level 3. During this course i have learnt a magnitude of things that i could never learnt by myself, this includes things like 3D Modelling, Levelling, Texturing, 2D Pixel Art, among a magnitude of other things. Unfortunately, the one thing this course did not specialise in that well is coding, which I have taken the time to learn myself for the past 4-5 years at home. During this time i also learnt multiple other basic coding concepts just based on a blueprint game engine called construct. Construct has helped me learn not how to code but how to understand code better and how things in game engines work without me coding it, which has helped me outside of my college course in my personal work. It also allowed me to make this website with understanding how websites are made and the importance of some of the main mechanics / functions a website should include.
